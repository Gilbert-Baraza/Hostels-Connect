# Hostels Connect

**Connecting students to their perfect stay** — discover verified, affordable, and comfortable hostels near your university.  
Built with **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**, Hostels Connect makes it easy for students to find hostels and for landlords to manage listings efficiently.

---

## Features

###  Students
- Sign up and log in securely  
- Browse approved hostels  
- Book available hostels  
- Contact verified landlords  

### Landlords
- Register and log in  
- Add, edit, or remove hostel listings  
- Approve or reject booking requests  

### Admin
- Oversee all users (students + landlords)  
- Approve hostels for listing visibility  
- Maintain platform integrity
